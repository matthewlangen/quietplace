A QUIET PLACE

An interactive map showcasing the best places to study at Tufts

Using Seeed Wio Nodes and Grove sensors, we collected over 50 hours of loudness and crowdedness data from 15 of the most popular study spots on the Tufts University campus. The result is the map you see here. To read more about our methodology, data processing, findings, and the project as a whole, take a look at our report here.

Created by Will Glockner, Matthew Langen, and Tommaso Lombardi
